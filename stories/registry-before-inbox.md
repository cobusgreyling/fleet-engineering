# Registry Before Inbox — Honest Win

**Context:** 12 agents across Slack and IDE; no central list.  
**Pattern:** Team Agent Registry (F1)

## What worked

Two-hour inventory surfaced four duplicate "weekly report" agents with different credentials. Retired two; named owners for the rest.

## What we skipped (on purpose)

Shared Inbox — not until registry was complete. Inbox without registry = approving unknown actors.

## Lesson

Fleet engineering week one is boring catalog work. That is the point.
